Nestled by the Ganges, The Ffort Raichak brings you the harmonious union of nature, luxury and comfort to renew your body, mind and soul.

Designed to give the look and feel of a heritage fort, The Ffort Raichak will compel you to indulge in a different world all together! Blending nature and history, it is the perfect luxurious escape to transcend into the extraordinary. The old narrow bricks, treasure chests used as tables, swords, the grand piano and the splendid view of the river are a welcome change from the usual. So enjoy the pool, luxurious living in the magnificent citadel and the splendid sunsets as you dive in to find yourself in nature!
