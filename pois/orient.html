Ambience Oriental

Stepping into The Orient you will find yourself entranced by its East-Asian ambience. The exquisite patterns and red and back theme of the restaurant along with its distinctly oriental tableware and ancient chests and pots will all add to your exceptional experience of eating here. The ceiling has a unique blend of oriental dialects that may pique your interest and have you decipher it as you savour your tasteful meal.

Cuisine Oriental

Holidays are meant for feasting on the food you love so if authentic oriental food is your weakness it’s time to give in and allow the flavours to burst in your mouth at Orient! From spicy chicken wings and hot woks to more gentle gravy noodles, Orient can cater to all kinds of palates. The vegetarian dumpling, mango pudding with kiwi and hot chocolate muffin with ice cream are some things that will keep getting you back here time and again.

Signature Dish Tuna wasabi nigiri

The Orient serves you the most authentic delectable Japanese delicacy, Tuna wasabi nigiri, as its signature dish. A beautifully curated platter decorated with the most elegant Japanese one-biter, it’s a flavour bomb waiting to explode in your mouth. The Japanese sour sushi rice, layered with a hint of wasabi and crushed black peppers will make you feel a tingling heat on your palate. It’s topped off with a delicate piece of high-quality tuna bound by a thin strip of nori. All you’ll want to say after tasting it is Arigato gozaimasu (thank you)!
