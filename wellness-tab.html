Signature Treatment Shirodhara

Stress has become a part of our lifestyle, and while Sanjeeva Spa might not be able to take that all away their signature treatment ‘shirodhara’ makes attempts to sooth it. ‘Shiro’ means liquid, ‘dhara’ means flow and as its name suggest, it is a therapy which involves a mixture of essential oil being poured on the forehead while you lay flat on clinically designed beds. This healing process is followed by an immaculate head massage by their experts which facilitates the blood flow in your head.

Signature Treatment Hot Potli

Small heated herbal pouches (potli) comprising various medicinal herbs ease stress in joints and muscles and that is precisely what this massage is most useful for. The ingredients in the potlis have varied benefits. Ashwagandha, the king of all herbs is used to relieve stress and relax sore muscles when heated and touched upon the skin. Mustard and neem both release essential oils which are used to detoxify the skin and body. Turmeric and ginger are used to rejuvenate and cleanse the body whereas Rosemary and rice improve blood circulation, therefore, abating stress. The hot potli is a timeless therapy which is highly effective till date.
