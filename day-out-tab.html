What is Bargain Holiday?

InstaHoliay is India’s leading online travel marketplace bringing both the travelers, and trusted & expert travel agents on a common platform. While several other established players in the market have been offering holiday packages, what makes InstaHoliay stand out from the crowd is that it is the only platform which solves the complexity of holiday planning through technology and expertise.

On receiving the holiday request, multiple expert travel agents first understand the traveler needs in detail and then provide quote listing the itinerary and price. Further, we let the traveler compare the multiple quotes and figure out the best option for himself/herself.
