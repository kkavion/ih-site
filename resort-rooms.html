  3. The room will not be retained beyond 8 pm. in case of late check-in unless informed at the Reservation Department at 9831404040 or by email at reservation@neotiahospitality.com regarding late arrival
  4. Photo ID Proofs for the guests is mandatory. The same needs to be shown at the time of security check at Resort.
  5. Check-in is at 1:00 PM and Check-out at 11 AM.
  6. Early check-in charges:
    1. Early check-in at 9-11 AM subject to availability at ₹ 1,000 per room
    2. Early check-in at 7-9 AM subject to availability at ₹ 2,000 per room
    3. Early check-in before 7 AM subject to availability at full day charge per room
  7. Late check-out will be on sole discretion of Front Office Manager and the approval for the same has to be taken at the time of check-in subject to availability. However, the following charges would be levied.
    1. 11:00 AM to 3 PM 25% of the room tariff.
    2. 3.00 PM onwards full day charge of the room tariff.
    3. On failure of the Guest to vacate the room on expiry of the check-out time mentioned in the confirmation voucher, Management shall have the right to remove the Guest and his/her belongings from the assigned room/s.

FAQs

What is TravelTriangle?

TravelTriangle is India’s leading online travel marketplace bringing both the travelers, and trusted & expert travel agents on a common platform. While several other established players in the market have been offering holiday packages, what makes TravelTriangle stand out from the crowd is that it is the only platform which solves the complexity of holiday planning through technology and expertise. On receiving the holiday request, multiple expert travel agents first understand the traveler needs in detail and then provide quote listing the itinerary and price. Each travel agent is personally and methodically verified by us. Their ratings and traveler reviews are also shared with the traveler, so that transparency is maintained. Further, we let the traveler compare the multiple quotes and figure out the best option for himself/herself. The traveler then has the option to customize the holiday package the way he/she wants through call or message on the dashboard itself. We manage the entire fulfilment process ensuring the traveler gets what he/she has been promised. We offer great value prices coupled with convenient payment solutions, such as EMIs and partial payment on arrival.
