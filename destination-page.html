Overview

Passionate about adventuring into the mountains? Here’s your chance of witnessing the mountains and nature for what it truly is, beautiful and pure! Whether be it heli-skiing in the Himalayas, paragliding in Bir/Himachal Pradesh, river rafting in Coorg, bunjee jumping in Rishikesh or zip lining in Mussoorie, zorbing in Nainital, mountain biking in the trails of Sikkim and trekking in Dodital/Uttarakhandl, all provide the much desired experience of thrill and adventure! Must Do: Heli-Skiing in India: Gulmarg (Kashmir), Paragliding in India: Stok Kangri (Ladakh), River Rafting in India: Manali, Mountain Biking in India: Pang to Rumtse (Ladakh), Destinations for Trekking in India: Araku Valley, Zip lining in India: Buranshkanda, Zorbing in India: Bhimtal
