What is Surprise Holiday?

InstaHoliay is India’s leading online travel marketplace bringing both the travelers, and trusted & expert travel agents on a common platform. While several other established players in the market have been offering holiday packages, what makes InstaHoliay stand out from the crowd is that it is the only platform which solves the complexity of holiday planning through technology and expertise.

On receiving the holiday request, multiple expert travel agents first understand the traveler needs in detail and then provide quote listing the itinerary and price. Each travel agent is personally and methodically verified by us. Their ratings and traveler reviews are also shared with the traveler, so that transparency is maintained.

Further, we let the traveler compare the multiple quotes and figure out the best option for himself/herself. The traveler then has the option to customize the holiday package the way he/she wants through call or message on the dashboard itself. We manage the entire fulfilment process ensuring the traveler gets what he/she has been promised. We offer great value prices coupled with convenient payment solutions, such as EMIs and partial payment on arrival.
