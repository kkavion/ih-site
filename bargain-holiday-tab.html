  2. Confirmation Voucher is non-transferable and is valid only if signed by the concerned authority.
  3. The room will not be retained beyond 8 pm. in case of late check-in unless informed at the Reservation Department at 9831404040 or by email at reservation@neotiahospitality.com regarding late arrival
  4. Photo ID Proofs for the guests is mandatory. The same needs to be shown at the time of security check at Resort.
  5. Check-in is at 1:00 PM and Check-out at 11 AM.
  6. Early check-in charges:
    1. Early check-in at 9-11 AM subject to availability at Rs 1,000 per room
    2. Early check-in at 7-9 AM subject to availability at Rs 2,000 per room
    3. Early check-in before 7 AM subject to availability at full day charge per room
  7. Late check-out will be on sole discretion of Front Office Manager and the approval for the same has to be taken at the time of check-in subject to availability. However, the following charges would be levied.
    1. 11:00 AM to 3 PM 25% of the room tariff.
    2. 3.00 PM onwards full day charge of the room tariff.
    3. On failure of the Guest to vacate the room on expiry of the check-out time mentioned in the confirmation voucher, Management shall have the right to remove the Guest and his/her belongings from the assigned room/s.

  9. The Management will not in any way whatsoever be responsible for any loss and/or damage to Guests belongings or any other property from either the room or locker, or any other part of the hotel for any cause whatsoever including theft or pilferage.
