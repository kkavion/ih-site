Ambience Sports Bar cum Lounge

Footloose is a sports bar cum lounge where you can allow yourself to loosen up to the pulsating beats of the music and groove to it while having some delicious pizzas and sipping any drink of your choice. The white curtains, tray lights, plush dark sofas, high bar stools, hanging glass balls and the disco light create an ambience that will compel you to forget all your worries and get into a party mood!

It reserves a special space for those to love to lose themselves to the music of the night. If you’re not much of a dancer but love to enjoy the vibe nonetheless, you can find artfully designed seating areas that will allow you a good view of the dance floor. The discotheque is open all evening except Mondays but if you want to enjoy a private evening with your family and friends, all you need to do is book it in advance.

Food

Whether you’re there to dance or revel in the upbeat atmosphere, one thing that will keep you company and revive your stamina is the delicious grub served. Gobble up the enticing savouries and get going to the dance floor!
