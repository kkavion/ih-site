“With the backdrop of the majestic Ffort it seems the perfect place for a wedding/conference!”

The Ffort Raichak invites you to have an invigorating experience while you savour the day that marks the beginning of your togetherness with your loved ones. It offers you the complete package anyone could ask for with its infinity fools, upbeat lounges, multiple refined dining outlets, sprawling lawns and banquets than can host all your guests along with the warmth and tranquility that only nature can provide.

Ambience 40,000 sft

The North Lawn is the most impressive one having the capacity to seat over 700 guests! With the backdrop of the majestic Ffort it seems the perfect place to set up an outdoor frame tent complete with drapery, vibrant flowers, crystal chandeliers and scented candles. If you’re more of a dancer and enjoy performances while swaying to the beats then setting up a stage with focused lighting and a customised dance floor is the best way to dance your night away to the rhythm of the music.

Conference

For those who seem to work best when outdoors, the extensive North Lawns provide a space where workshops and team-building exercises can take place to help them bond. Setting up a marquee tent with all its flair and indulging in group discussions while being served savouries to relish can only serve to heighten the morale of the attendees and make the conference a success!

Ideal for: Sangeet, Reception, Cocktail Party
