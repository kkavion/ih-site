The beauty of the resort is in its eco-friendly natural structure & ambience. Ram Shyam Village Resort welcomes you to the Red land of Rich Culture and beauty of Sonajhuri (Khoai)!!

In a distance of about 1.5 km from Shantiniketan, amidst Khoai and Sonajhuri you get to see Ram Shyam Village Resort in a pleasant natural surroundings. You can enjoy an excellent stay along with delicious Bengali cuisine of Birbhum. It also has the basic facilities of AC, generator, geyser and awesome hospitality from the in-house team of the resort. Come and do experience your stay in a village environment! The red land of Sonajhuri is mesmerising in its own way. Its freshness, peace and tranquillity will rejuvenate you from your daily stressful lives. The resort offers you a classic Bengali food served in a traditional way (kansar thali) with delicious items. It has a wide arrangement of entertainments from Rabindra Sangeet, Rabindrik Dance, and tribal dance to Bengali sufi dance & music as well.

FAQs

Why should I visit Raichak?

Raichak is well known for its picturesque setting by the Ganges and its property offerings. The leisure activities, together with all its refined eating outlets and spa treatments are what draw in the numbers. All in all, Raichak doesn’t provide you with one, but many reasons to experience its hospitality.

What is the fastest way to get to Raichak? What is the nearest landmark?

The fastest way to reach Raichak is by car or cab via the NH12 and NH117.

What activities can I do in Raichak?

Raichak presents manifold activities to indulge in. The most sought-after are cruising in the Pari, enjoying games, sports and swimming in Club Rio and clubbing in Footloose. You can choose to do them all and more to suit your hobbies.

What are the accepted payment modes?

Payment at the resort can be made via cash or credit card.

Where should I eat in Raichak?

When in Raichak, do try out international and oriental delicacies at Reflection and The Orient. If you are a lover of traditional Bengali fare, the Zamindari Thali at Sonar Tori will surely blow your mind!

Can the banquets and lawns be hired for day use only?

Banquets and lawns are available for day use both with and without rooms. Rates can be contracted by enquiring at their office.
