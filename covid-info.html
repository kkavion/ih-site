COVID-19 Information

The wake of this pandemic has left a huge impact on the global tourism scene and although we’re almost past it, we must keep in mind our safety and that of those around us as well. Travelling once again can prove beneficial for your revival but not when your health is at stake. So do make sure to abide by the COVID-19 protocols and resume your voyage of exploration once you’ve secured both your vaccinations. To ensure that your holidays are not only pleasant but also safe, live by the mantra, ‘Distance and Mask are not much wealth but will go far to preserve/conserve/safeguard/protect your health!’

For more information, please visit: Ministry of Health and Family Welfare | West Bengal State Portal
