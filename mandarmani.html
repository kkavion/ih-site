Mandarmani

A long stretch of the sandy beach with coconut groves all around, the seamless sea and thrilling water sports are what Mandarmani has to offer. The serene atmosphere of the sea and the seemingly unending stretch of one of the longest motorable beaches in Mainland Asia abounding in venturous activities will have a rush of adrenaline pumping into your veins and liberate you by imbuing solace into your soul. A tranquil seaside resort village in West Bengal, Mandarmani gives you a chance to enjoy unrestrained tranquillity and solitude and the perfect respite from the chaos and din of city life.

Trivia

Passionate about adventuring into the mountains? Here’s your chance of witnessing the mountains and nature for what it truly is, beautiful and pure! Whether be it heli-skiing in the Himalayas.

Indulgence

What must be done while you’re in Mandarmani is to experience the beach and the eternal waters in all their glory while you indulge in gratuitous watersports and fill your stomach with desired delights till the exhaustion of the city life wears out of you and all that remains is an ecstatic joy that never wears off!

Best time to travel

Monsoon conjures the ideal atmosphere and the beach looks ethereal. It's the peak season here as it offers a mesmerizing view of the beach and the rain. The sight is spectacular and accommodations are sparse. However, the aesthetically appealing view attracts people from all over. Cotton clothes and swimwear are advisable to wear in Mandarmani. It is mandatory to carry an umbrella and sunscreen to avoid sunburn. Appropriate clothes will only help you to achieve optimum enjoyment and relaxation.

Mandarmani Beach 2,450 sq ft

Mandarmani | West Bengal 721455

Situated in the East Midnapore district of West Bengal, Mandarmani is estimated to have the longest motorable beach in India, around 13 km long. The most quirky aspect of this beachside is the sight of red crabs crawling alongside the beach, making it look like a shimmering field full of red flowers. This appeals to the imagination of the residents. Formerly known as Mandar, it, later on, changed its name to Mandarboni and then Mandarmani. A hit as a holiday destination amongst tourists, the serene tropical seaside breeze here, surely melts hearts and refreshes minds.

Destination Specialty 2,450 sq ft

Mandarmani | West Bengal 721455

Dab Chingri is an exquisite Bengali delicacy with shrimp cooked in tender coconut cream (malai), mustard paste and other authentic Bengali spices. The velvety smooth gravy, tingling heat of the mustard and sweet shrimps are bound to fulfil your craving for an authentic Bengali dish. Another crowd favourite is the Bagda Chingri'r Malaikari made of lobster or prawns in coconut milk. This creamy yet surprisingly light gravy is scented with Bengali ‘gota gorom moshla’ (whole spices) and flavoured with ginger, fresh green chilli, and, of course, ‘shorsher tel’ (mustard oil). If you’re eager for some crab then you’re sure to love the Kakra’r Jhal, which as its name suggests is a spicy crab curry made of hot spices, dried chilli powder, fresh chillies and juicy potatoes as it will put your tongue on fire but surely soothe your soul!
